Welcome to my website on clean and renewable energy data visualization!

As the demand for clean and renewable energy continues to increase, it's important to have a clear understanding of the current state and potential of these technologies. Data visualization can be a powerful tool in this regard, helping to make complex and often large datasets more accessible and easier to understand. Here, I present visually appealing and informative data visualizations on a wide range of clean and renewable energy sources, including solar, wind, hydroelectric, geothermal, and more. I strive to present the data in a way that is both engaging and accurate that helps to put the numbers into perspective. Please use the tools available to explore and have fun while learning about energy!
